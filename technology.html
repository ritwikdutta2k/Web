The Rise of Quantum Computing

Quantum computing is poised to revolutionize the tech industry . Indeed, quantum computing holds the potential to revolutionize the tech industry in several ways. Quantum computing differs fundamentally from classical computing, which relies on bits to represent either a 0 or a 1. In contrast, quantum computers use quantum bits or qubits, which can exist in multiple states simultaneously due to the principles of superposition and entanglement.
